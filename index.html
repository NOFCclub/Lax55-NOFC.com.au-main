About NOFC

Our story, mission, and values

Our Heritage

Founded in 2021, NOFC represents the Nepalese community in Orange, NSW, bringing traditional values to modern football.

Community First

We believe in building strong community bonds through the beautiful game, fostering friendship and cultural exchange.

Excellence

Striving for excellence on and off the field, developing players and contributing to local football culture.
